## Problem Statement

This dashboard helps ElectroHub analyze its overall sales performance and business growth.
It enables stakeholders to understand sales trends, profitability, product performance, discounts, and customer behavior across different regions and time periods.

By using this dashboard, the organization can:

- Identify top and bottom performing products
- Analyze sales and profit trends over time
- Understand the impact of discounts on sales and profit
- Compare performance between different time periods
- Evaluate city-wise sales contribution

These insights help management take data-driven decisions to improve revenue, optimize pricing strategies, and enhance operational efficiency.

### Steps followed 

- Step 1 : Loaded sales dataset into "Power BI Desktop".
- Step 2 : Opened "Power Query Editor" and enabled:
   - Column distribution
   - Column quality
   - Column profile
- Step 3 : Enabled "column profiling based on the entire dataset".
- Step 4 : Cleaned and validated data by handling missing and inconsistent values.
- Step 5 : Created relationships between tables for effective "data modeling".
- Step 6 : Designed report layout and selected a suitable theme.
- Step 7 : Created visuals to show:
   - Top & Bottom 5 Products by Sales, Profit, and Quantity
   - Sales trends (Daily, Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly) 
- Step 8 : Added slicers for dynamic filtering:
   - Product
   - Date
   - Customer ID
   - Promotion Category
- Step 9 : Created visuals to analyze the relationship between Sales and Profit.
- Step 10 : Implemented period comparison to compare Sales, Profit, and Quantity between two selected time periods.
- Step 11 : Created visuals for:
   - Average discount by discount category 
   - Total number of orders
- Step 12 : Designed order-level detailed table showing:
   - Sales
   - Profit
   - discount
   - Net Sales
- Step 13 : Added city-wise sales analysis using map and bar visuals.
- Step 14 :Created DAX measures for key KPIs such as:
        - Total Sales
        - Total Profit
        - Total Orders
        - Net Sales
- Step 15 : Finalized dashboard with proper formatting and alignment.

# Insights

A single-page interactive dashboard was created using Power BI.
The following insights were derived from the analysis:

### [1] Product Performance

- Identified Top 5 and Bottom 5 products based on:
   - Sales
   - Profit
   - Quantity Sold
- Helped in recognizing high-performing and underperforming products.
           
### [2] Sales Trends

- Sales trends analyzed across:
  - Daily
  - Monthly
  - Quarterly
  - Yearly
- Seasonal patterns and growth trends were observed.
  
  ### [3] Sales vs Profit Relationship 

- Visualized correlation between Sales and Profit.
- Identified products with high sales but low profit, indicating pricing or discount issues.

 ### [4] Discount Analysis
- Calculated average discount across different discount categories.
- Observed impact of discounts on overall profitability.

### [5] Orders Analysis
- Calculated total number of orders.
- Displayed detailed order-level data with filters for deep analysis.

### [6] City-wise Sales Performance
- Analyzed sales distribution across different cities.
- Identified cities contributing the highest revenue.

### [7] Period Comparison
- Compared Sales, Profit, and Quantity between two selected periods.
- Enabled quick performance comparison for business evaluation.
